Chaingang Club

pedals, chains and Lungs

The Chaingang Cycling Club Abuja, is Nigeria's foremost mountain biking and off-road cycling family located in Abuja.

Vision

The vision of the Chaingang Cycling Club Abuja is to see a world that embraces off-road cyclingas a means of promoting physical wellness,unity and social development.

Mission

Our mission is to promote off-road cycling as a sport, lifestyle and community while supporting the broader cycling association in Nigeria.

OUR OBJECTIVES

To provide recreational bicycling activities and rides for mountain bikers within and around Abuja.

To defend and protect the rights of bicyclists; to secure a better understanding and recognition of the need for safer riding conditions.

To provide an opportunity for members to socialize with others who have an interest in bicycling, and promote health and fitness through cycling

To promote mountain biking and cycling generally; to encourage and facilitate touring, bicycle outings or competitions, and all forms of recreational bicycling activities

OUR VALUES

As fast as the slowest rider

We abhor discrimination of any type, whether it be on issues of fitness levels, gender, political and religious leanings or socio-economic status. We endeavor to carry one another along,on or off the trail.

Leave no man behind

Closely linked to the first point above, is our commitment to leave no one behind on our trails. This has fostered a sense of family, comradeship and community growth

Protect the environment

We are a pro-environment group. We actively discourage littering of the environment,and we encourage environmental protection, climate action and tourism for development.

Build the community

Our rides give us a rare intimate peak in to the condition of under-developed communities along our riding trails. Each year we commit to undertaking development projects that build and develop these communities and support their inhabitants.
